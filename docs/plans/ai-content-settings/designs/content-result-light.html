About Books to Scrape

A quiet corner of the web for readers who’d rather browse a well-kept shelf than scroll an endless feed.

Books to Scrape started as a simple idea: make it easy to find your next read without the noise. We stock more than a thousand titles across fiction, non-fiction, poetry, and children’s books, each with a clear rating, an honest price, and a stock count you can actually rely on.

Why readers keep coming back

  • Straight-talking listings. Every book shows its real availability and star rating — no inflated reviews, no surprises at checkout.
  • Browse by mood, not just by category. From travel writing to mystery, our shelves are organised the way readers actually think.
  • Prices that make sense. Clear pricing on every title, updated as stock changes.

Our promise

We’re a small team that cares about the reading experience end to end — from the first search to the moment a parcel lands on your doorstep. If a title is listed, it’s in stock; if it’s rated, that rating comes from real readers. Browse the full fiction collection to see what’s in stock today.
